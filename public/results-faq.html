Results FAQs

What are the results?
This is a listing of the elections that match your search criteria.

How can I limit the number of results?
You can go back to the advanced search page and use more detailed search terms.

What do I do if there are no results listed?
Go back to the search page and change your search. You may have put in to many search requirements. Or you may have misspelled an item in the keyword search. You may also use the browsing section to look through all of the available elections. See Browse Help.

How do I view the election records?
Click on the title of the election, and the election record will load.

How do I view more results?
Use the arrow next to the page number or you may be able to view more results on the same page.
