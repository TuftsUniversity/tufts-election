Results FAQs
What are the results?

This is a listing of the elections that match your search criteria.

How can I limit the number of results?

Expand the facets in the left-hand navigation, and select terms to help you narrow your search. For example, if you are only interested in collections from Pennsylvania, expand "State" and select "Pennsylvania" to narrow your search.

What do I do if there are no results listed?

Go back to the search page and change your search. You may have put in to many search requirements. You may have misspelled an item in the keyword search. You may also use the browsing section to look through all of the available elections. See Browse FAQs.

How do I view the election records?

Select the title of the election, and the election record will load.

How do I view the election records in a separate window?

Right-click on the title the election that you would like to see. Depending on your browser you will see an option that says something like "open in a new tab" or "open in a new window". If you have a three button mouse, the middle button will often open the election in a new window or tab.

How do I view more results?

At the top of your search results, you will see an option for "show [number of results] for page".
